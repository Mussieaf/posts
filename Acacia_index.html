About Acacia Trading

We cultivate experiences that help your business grow.

Like the Acacia tree that defines our landscape, we are resilient, deeply rooted, and provide a gathering place for communities. Based in the heart of Addis Ababa, Acacia Trading is a full-service event promotion and management company.

Whether you are launching a new product in Bole, hosting an international conference at the AU, or organizing a cultural festival, we bridge the gap between your brand and the Ethiopian audience with cultural intelligence and logistical precision.
