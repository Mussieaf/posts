Luxury Resort Launch

Introducing a new standard of leisure to the Ethiopian hospitality landscape.

The Project

Acacia Trading was commissioned to organize the grand opening of the Acacia Resort, a premium lakeside destination. Our goal was to blend modern luxury with traditional Ethiopian hospitality to attract both international tourists and local dignitaries.

The launch event featured a guided tour of the villas, a sunset cocktail hour by the infinity pool, and a ribbon-cutting ceremony attended by government officials. We managed the entire guest list, catering logistics, and media coverage.

Key Achievements

  • Hosted 300+ VIP guests including ambassadors and influencers.
  • Secured coverage in 5 major national media outlets.
  • Orchestrated a seamless live music performance by a renowned Jazz band.
