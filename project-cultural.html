Venue Design

Cultural Heritage Gala

Celebrating Ethiopia's rich history through immersive event design.

The Vision

For the Annual Cultural Heritage Gala, we transformed a modern hall into a breathtaking tribute to Ethiopian artistry. The focal point was a massive custom backdrop featuring traditional motifs, designed to frame the keynote speakers and cultural performances perfectly.

Our team handled the interior staging, lighting design, and acoustic setup. We utilized warm, earthy tones and grand chandeliers to create an atmosphere of sophistication and respect for tradition.

Design Highlights

  • Custom 10-meter hand-painted backdrop.
  • Integrated stage lighting to highlight artifacts.
  • Seating arrangement for 500 guests with optimal sightlines.
