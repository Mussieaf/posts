The Expo

This outdoor exhibition focused on the future of transport in Ethiopia. We partnered with "Globo Hydro Power" to showcase their hydrogen-powered trucks. The challenge was to create an engaging outdoor setup that could accommodate heavy machinery while providing comfortable networking areas.

Acacia Trading managed the site layout, safety protocols for vehicle demonstrations, and the branding of the trucks. We also coordinated B2B meetings between logistics providers and government regulators.

Impact

  • Successfully demonstrated hydrogen fuel technology to 100+ industry leaders.
  • Facilitated 20+ partnership agreements on-site.
  • Zero safety incidents during live vehicle demos.
